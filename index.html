About our IOT solution.

The proposed IoT solution is designed to address critical challenges in the field of organ transportation. It comprises a Raspberry Pi, a GPS tracker, a temperature and humidity sensor, and a camera. This system ensures the safe transportation of organs and verifies that they are in the appropriate condition upon delivery to the end user.


The need for this solution arises from the sensitive nature of organ transportation. Organs must be kept within specific temperature and humidity ranges to maintain their viability for transplantation. Additionally, tracking the organ’s location in real-time is crucial to ensure timely delivery, as any delay can result in the organ becoming unusable.
